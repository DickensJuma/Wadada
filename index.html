Why we Built Wadada

To improve the lives of women by making high-quality care accessible and convenient.

Wadada is a digital space connecting women to professionals that help them tackle major issues that hinder the growth and development of women in general,be it home or workspace.

The program Is supposed to target key women health issues;post partum depression,pre natal services,post rape procedures,polycystic ovary syndrome,cervical and breast cancer,cyber bullying and depression,just to mention a few.

This platform was built in an attempt to provide instant ,readily available ,reliable and personalised resources to women to enable them be more productive in the society.

Wadada platform is focused on keeping women physical and mental health in check.by providing an ineractive space where women can share experiences and get professional feedback for free.
